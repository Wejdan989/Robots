Medical robots: They help in performing surgical and therapeutic tasks with greater accuracy and safety. They are used in hospitals to assist doctors and surgeons in surgeries such as hip replacement, which reduces the risk of complications and improves surgical results.

Industrial robots: They help produce more products with fewer workers. They are used in car factories to perform routine tasks such as welding and assembling car parts, allowing companies to produce more cars at a lower cost.

Military robots: They help in performing combat missions and protecting soldiers from danger, as they are used to fire, transport ammunition, and search for mines.
